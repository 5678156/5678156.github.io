SourDough Bread Starter
  • A good sourdough bread requires an active starter.
  • A starter is simply fermented flour and water.
  • Once you have an active starter, you must feed it daily to keep it active.
  • Feeding just means giving it equal parts flour and water and letting it sit on the counter to ferment.
  • You can place it in the fridge until you need to use it.
