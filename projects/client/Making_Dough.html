Making Dough
  • To make sourdough bread, you need some basic equipment.
  • You will need:
  • a kitchen scale
  • a thermometer
  • a mixing bowl
  • a dutch oven (or a covered pot that can be heated to over 450 degrees)
  • Using your kitchen scale, mix the following ingredients together in your mixing bowl to make a sticky dough.
  • 100 grams of fed and active sourdough starter
  • 450 grams of unbleached flour (any kind)
  • 300 grams of water
  • 10 grams of salt
  • Mix everything in your mixing bowl for about 2-5 minute.
    • It will be very sticky.

Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
